The Miyama Philosophy

INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY

Bridging the gap between human intuition and quantitative discipline to pursue compounding, not heroics.

Macro Regime Adaptive

We don't predict; we adapt. Whether in inflation shocks or deflationary busts, we utilize a flexible multi-asset framework—from equities to rates and FX carry—to capture alpha across changing market regimes.

Grounded in real-world experience dynamically shifting exposures across macro cycles instead of anchoring to a single narrative.

Asymmetric Risk Control

Survival precedes profit. Our strategy is built on strict drawdown limits, liquidity awareness, and predefined exit rules. Stop-losses are treated not as failures, but as the premium paid for long-term compounding.

Hard risk limits and position sizing are designed around staying in the game, not optimizing every single trade.

Quantamental Edge

Bridging the gap between human intuition and machine precision. By leveraging AI-powered sentiment and positioning analysis across Asian and US markets, we identify crowding risks before they evolve into full-scale reversals.

Combining a discretionary macro framework with AI tools to proactively adjust risk when sentiment and positioning reach extremes.

FOUNDER'S VISION

In a world of noise, clarity comes from discipline.

“In a world of noise, clarity comes from discipline. We don't strive to be right every time; we strive to survive every scenario and thrive in the trends that matter.”

In noisy markets, discipline is the only controllable edge. We do not aim to be correct on every position; instead, we use rigorous risk management and dynamic regime awareness to protect capital during systemic stress and deploy risk into high-conviction macro trends when probabilities are skewed in our favor.

Kuan Hsun Wang
Founder & CIO, Miyama Capital
